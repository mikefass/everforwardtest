ATSC 3.0 / NEXTGEN TV Bound

Updated: January 6, 2022

How could we spend a week highlighting the various needs for new technologies and workflows without mentioning ATSC 3.0 / NEXTGEN TV?! For those of you not familiar with NEXTGEN TV, it is the latest over-the-air platform that allows broadcasters to provide a more interactive and targeted service to their viewers. What does that mean? It means everything. It means you may have an app-based station available to viewers so they have a choice of what program to watch. It means you can send information and data to your audience that they can download and access. It means when emergencies occur, broadcasters can specifically target certain areas for safety. All via an over-the-air signal. The possibilities aren’t even fully defined yet, and the potential is astounding.


Have you recently begun, or are you planning to implement ATSC 3.0 / NEXTGEN TV? This is an exciting opportunity for your station, and will offer an enhanced viewing experience for your audience. You might be wondering what we could bring to the table as consultants working for you towards your NEXTGEN TV goals..and the answer is workflow development. The vendor relationships we’ve created allow us to stay on top of all the developments on the engineering side of this new technology. But along with launching NEXTGEN TV comes opportunities to build new operational workflows that better position your team to take full advantage of NEXTGEN TV and how your content and advertising is delivered. The opportunities are just beginning to reveal themselves, and we’d love the opportunity to discuss your future with NEXTGEN TV.
