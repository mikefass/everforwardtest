About


Lisa J. Guill

Lisa is the founder of EVERForward Strategies, LLC. She is a proven business and technology leader, and a project manager skilled with connecting the dots. With over 25 years of leadership experience as Operations Manager and General Manager, she brings a wealth of knowledge and resources. Lisa is passionate about working for each individual involved in any project and provides a clear, foreseeable vision that will allow a daunting project to become obtainable. She thrives in helping businesses solve workflow and technology challenges. Strategic when bringing a team together, Lisa helps to chart a path with defined milestones and then navigates in a way that allows your team to see the goal line.



Mike Fass

Mike is a highly skilled multimedia technician, excelling in creative and efficient workflows for best delivery paths from acquisition to distribution. In addition, you will find that he is proficient with live and recorded studio productions including camera blocking, lighting, and automation. Over Mike’s career, he has had to be evolutionary, and when needed, be revolutionary. He is genuinely empathetic towards those that are responsible for working in the trenches and understands how new technology can be exciting but, also intimidating. Mike is excellent at instilling a calm in what can be a very challenging transition and approaches every project with new energy.
